ABOUT THE INTERNSHIP

The internship is designed to surface the very best of candidates out of a late pool of participants. It is fast paced and challenging, and helps people learn how to work in teams , deal with pressure and quickly expand their knowledge.

Who Should Apply

Anybody can apply. The Internship itself focuses on participants
with prior experience either by going through the training phases,
or self learning. However, if you have no prior experience, we still
encourage you to apply ,you'll learn a lot from the experience.
